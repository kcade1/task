Biking

Bike riding uses minimal fossil fuels and is a pollution-free mode of transport. Bikes reduce the need to build, service and dispose of cars. Cycling 10 km each way to work would save 1500 kg of greenhouse gas emissions each year.Biking is also a great way to get exercise and bikes are relatively cheap

Recycling

Did you know that un recycled glass can take up to a million years to decompose? Recycling glass can reduce related water pollution by up to 50% and related air pollution by up to 20%. Recycling also
-Reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators.
-Increases economic security by tapping a domestic source of materials.
-Prevents pollution by reducing the need to collect new raw materials.
-Saves energy.
