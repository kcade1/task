Robotics



Let's face it, Robots are everywhere!
They are at your house, In your workplace and are even in space! Robotics are a essential part of STEM (Science,Technolgy,Engineering and Math).
By building robots kids learn the essential skills necessary for jobs.
The really cool thing about robotics is being part of a team working together to succeed, All skills are welcome and needed. It is not just about building robots but learning new skills, making freinds, having fun and more!

Here are a couple of the more popular competitions



FIRST Competitions

FIRST or (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technolgy) was founded in 1989. From little robots to human size giants, there have a varity of catagories targeted to different age groups.

Click here to learn more.

WRO (World Robot Olympiad)

WRO-USA is a world recognized international competition.
Children aged between 8 and 19 years can participate in one of the three competition categories: Regular, Open or Football Category.

Click here to learn more

Vex Competitions

VEX EDR and IQ catagories gives students the opportunity to apply their newfound STEM skills in an enthralling game-based engineering challenge.

Click here to learn more

BGCSTC (Boys and Girls Clubs of St.Charles)

If you don't want to start your own team, you can join one at Boys and Girls club. They have teams in each of the various catagories. At Boys and Girls Club, Kids can learn how to program, build, and design robots as early as 6
