Opportunities involving Kids with Disabilities
During the Summer Season

Lifebridge Camp

Lifebridge Camps are a great opportunity to kids with a documented disability to have fun in their community with other kids ages 6-18. There is camp during spring time, summer for St. Louis and St. Charles, and a winter break camp. This a great opporuntiy for kids as well as volunteering to help.

View Website

AMC Sensory Friendly Films

Parterning with the Autism Society, AMC shows films on certain days that have the lights up and the sound down that allow kids to move around during movies and have fun.

View Website

Riding for the Disabled

Camps available for kids with disabilities who can learn to foster a healthly lifestyle with therapeutic horseback riding! There are also opportunities for volunteering alongside the kids and horses.

View Website

We Rock the Spectrum

A fun safe, nurturing, and sensory friendly environment for all kids to grow in a new environment that has staff and volunteers that are trained for special care. Also has many locations around St. Louis!

View Website

Parks for All Kids

There are parks all around St. Louis that are specially built so that all kids can play alongside one another in the fun outdoors! .
